About me


Hello!

Thank you for coming here...



As a mom, I pour my heart into supporting the women and families I meet.
I hold an Honors degree from the University of London at SIM in Singapore, where i studied computer science, driven by its significance in the modern world.

While at university, I experienced an unexpected pregnancy, which led me to embrace motherhood while continuing my studies.

This period was filled with changes and anxieties, but it helped me to understand the incredible capabilities of my body and the power of a positive mindset in managing challenges.

With all the challenges I faced — People suggesting I should have an abortion, the father being absent and wanting the same, and dealing with financial stress. I really started to doubt what my body could handle. I knew the basics of labor from my studies, but I realized I needed to dig deeper and find more ways to prepare and support myself through this tough time.

This experience has deeply influenced my journey, particularly in supporting single parents. I wholeheartedly dedicate myself to caring for teens, women, and families, and I find great fulfillment in working across areas of maternity.
